FAQ

Pertanyaan yang sering diajukan. Temukan jawaban Anda di sini. Frequently asked questions. Find your answers here.

Jam berapa operasionalnya? What are the operational hours?

Kami buka setiap hari dari jam 07:00 pagi hingga 21:00 malam. We are open every day from 07:00 AM to 09:00 PM.

Berapa harga tiket masuk? How much is the entrance ticket?

Harga tiket adalah Rp 5.000 per orang. The ticket price is IDR 5,000 per person.

Apakah perlu reservasi? Is a reservation required?

Reservasi tidak wajib, namun sangat disarankan terutama saat akhir pekan atau hari libur untuk memastikan ketersediaan tempat. Reservations are not mandatory, but are highly recommended, especially on weekends or holidays, to ensure availability.
